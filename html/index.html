TCSS 491 Computational Worlds
Blue One
Prototype

Might take a little bit to load. Read the instructions while you're waiting!

This has an idle animation in 4 directions: forward, downward, left, right. It also has walking, sword swinging, shield raising, spell casting, and crossbow shooting in those same directions. If a spell is successfully cast it will create a fireball that animates in those 4 directions also.

Controls:
Left Mouse Click - Sword swing
W - Forward
A - Left
S - Downward
D - Right
E - Shield Raise
Space - Crossbow shoot
Q - Cast spell

*When casting a spell, the animation you see is an idle while the game waits for your input, in order to successfully cast the spell and see the fireball on the screen, you must type in the combo WWAD.*

Along with that, it has basic collisions and two different enemy types. The Plague Doctor will walk up to you until about 100 pixels, then it will cast a spell in your direction. The Screamer will just yell and run at you flailing it's arms. It has a melee tongue attack it does. There is a sound effect associated with the majority of the actions in the game (for the player and enemies).

Enemies are in the bottom right of the map and top left of the map as well

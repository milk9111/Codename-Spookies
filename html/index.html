Controls

  • W = Up
  • S = Down
  • A = Left
  • D = Right

Sword = Spacebar

Shield = E

Spells: Press Q then type in the combo

  • Fireball = WWAD
  • Freeze = WDAD
  • Light = SADW
  • Heal = ADSW

Your goal is to survive all three levels and find your way out of the darkness. There is a portal on each map that will teleport you to the next room. Getting hit by enemies will cause damage to your light, making it harder to see. Collect potions around the map to gain your light back.

Press F11 to go fullscreen and wear headphones for the best experience.

Fireball and Light spell are limited. You are given 3 light spells and 5 health spells per level. Use them carefully.
